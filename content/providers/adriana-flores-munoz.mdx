Adriana Flores is a graduate of Grand Valley State University. She earned her Bachelor of Science in Health Sciences in 2003 and Masters of Science in Physician Assistant Studies in 2006. She specialized her training in Asthma and Allergy with Dr. Ahmed Mohiuddin as a PA Student and joined our team at Allergy, Asthma and Sinus Centers in 2008. Adriana has a special interest in pediatrics and asthma. Her favorite part of being a physician assistant is educating patients and their parents so they have a better understanding of their disease and can take an active role in the treatment and control of their allergy and asthma symptoms. She takes time to get to know her patients and their individual needs to provide the best care plan for them.
