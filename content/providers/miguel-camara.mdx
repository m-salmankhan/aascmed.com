My philosophy of care is to treat each patient as a very important person, providing the best medical care with compassion, communication, and competence.

Accurate diagnosis and best treatment options for best patient outcomes is the goal for each patient encounter.

Patient education is vital for each to make decisions regarding their health.


## Education
* Medical School: University of The East Ramon Magsaysay Memorial Medical Center, Quezon City, Philippines, 1978-1982, Doctor of Medicine, (Ranked 2nd in class of 265).
* Internship: Makati Medical Center, Makati City, Philippines.
* Residency: Internal Medicine, University of Hawaii Integrated Medical Residency Training Program, Honolulu, Hawaii, June 1984 – June 1987.
* Fellowship: Allergy-Immunology at the National Jewish Center for Immunology &amp; Respiratory Medicine, Denver Colorado, July 1987 – June 1989.
